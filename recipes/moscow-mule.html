Moscow Mule Recipe

A Moscow Mule is a classic cocktail made with vodka, ginger beer, and fresh lime juice, served over ice in a signature copper mug. Its bold, spicy, and tangy flavor comes from the ginger beer, balanced by the citrusy brightness of lime, making it a refreshing yet zesty drink.

Ingredients

  • Vodka (2 ounces)
  • Ginger beer (4-6 ounces)
  • Lime juice (1/2 ounce)
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Use a copper mug (or any glass) for an authentic experience. Fill it halfway with ice cubes.
  2. Pour in the vodka and freshly squeezed lime juice.
  3. Fill the rest of the mug with ginger beer. Adjust the amount to your taste.
  4. Use a spoon to mix the ingredients, ensuring they blend evenly without losing too much carbonation.
  5. Add a lime wedge on the rim and, optionally, a sprig of fresh mint for a refreshing aroma.
