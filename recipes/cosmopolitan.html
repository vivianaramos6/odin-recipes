Cosmopolitan Recipe

A Cosmopolitan is a sophisticated and vibrant cocktail made with vodka, triple sec (like Cointreau), cranberry juice, and freshly squeezed lime juice. Known for its pink hue and tangy-sweet flavor, itâ€™s typically served in a chilled martini glass and garnished with a lime wheel or twist. A classic choice for a stylish evening!

Ingredients

  • Vodka (1.5 ounces, preferably citron vodka)
  • Triple Sec (0.75 ounce, like Cointreau)
  • Cranberry juice (0.5 ounce)
  • Fresh lime juice (0.25 ounce)

Steps

  1. Fill a shaker with ice: Add enough ice to cool the drink thoroughly.
  2. Pour in the vodka, triple sec, cranberry juice, and lime juice.
  3. Secure the shaker lid and shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds. This ensures the drink is well-chilled and mixed.
  4. Strain the mixture into a chilled martini glass.
  5. Garnish with a lime wheel or an orange twist on the rim of the glass for a stylish touch.
